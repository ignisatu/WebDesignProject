Timeline of European History from 1800 - 1945

Information and text sourced from havefunwithhistory

1815 - Congress of Vienna

The Congress of Vienna reorganizes Europe after the Napoleonic Wars, aiming to restore stability and prevent further conflicts.

1914 - World War 1

World War I erupts, involving many European powers. The war leads to immense destruction, loss of life, and the reshaping of political boundaries.

1917 - The Russian Revolution

The Russian Revolution overthrows the Romanov monarchy and establishes a communist government under Vladimir Lenin.

1939 - World War 2

World War II takes place, with Nazi Germany and its allies facing off against the Allied forces. The war results in widespread devastation, the Holocaust, and the eventual emergence of the United States and the Soviet Union as superpowers.
