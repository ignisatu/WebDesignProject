Timeline of Irish History from 1800 - 1945

Information and text sourced from wikipedia

1801 - Act of Union

Acts of Union 1800 comes into effect; the Kingdom of Ireland unites with Great Britain, forming the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland.

1845 - Irish Famine

Great Irish Famine: A potato blight destroys two-thirds of Ireland's staple crop, leading to an estimated 1 million deaths and emigration of a further 1 million people.

1886 - The 1st Home Rule Bill

1st Home Rule Bill, also known as the Government of Ireland Bill 1886.

1914 - The Government of Ireland Act

Government of Ireland Act is passed, providing for Irish Home Rule, but its application is simultaneously postponed for the duration of World War I..

1916 - The Easter Rising

Easter Rising: The Irish Republican Brotherhood leads an action which seizes key government buildings in Dublin, and issues the Proclamation of the Irish Republic. The Rising lasts til 29 April 1916.
